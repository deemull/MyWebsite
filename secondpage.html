I had played tuba for 7 years. I started when I was in 6th grade at William J. Fischer Accelerated Academy.

It wasn't easy at first, but once I learned the basic scales, everything else was easier to pick up from there.

I participated in Mardi Gras from 2009-2015. It was some of the most memorable years of my life. I would not trade it for the world.
